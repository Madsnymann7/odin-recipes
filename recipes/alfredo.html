Chicken Alfredo

Description

A rich and comforting pasta dish with tender chicken, velvety Alfredo sauce, and Parmesan, perfect for a quick yet indulgent meal.

Ingredients

  • 300 g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta: Boil fettuccine until al dente; drain.
  2. Cook chicken: Saut√© chicken in butter until golden; season.
  3. Make sauce: Add garlic, cream, and Parmesan; simmer until creamy.
  4. Combine: Toss pasta in sauce, top with extra Parmesan, and serve.
