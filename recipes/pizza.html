Pizza

Description

A simple yet flavorful pizza with a crisp crust, fresh tomato sauce, creamy mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 200 g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven: Set oven to 250°C (480°F) and prepare baking tray or pizza stone.
  2. Assemble: Spread tomato sauce on dough, add mozzarella and drizzle with olive oil..
  3. Bake: Cook for 8–10 minutes until cheese bubbles and crust is golden.
  4. Finish: Top with fresh basil, season with salt and pepper, and serve hot.
